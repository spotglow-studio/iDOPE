Artist & Producer

Leo Pynk

Afrobeats · Afropop · Production

Born Iko Sunday in Edo State, Nigeria, Leo Pynk started singing at school and turned music into a career right out of Moshood Abiola Polytechnic in 2019. A versatile artist and producer, he blends Afrobeats and Afropop into his own distinct sound. Known for crafting the beat behind Dantiti's "Big Big Tinz" — originally made for Davido — Leo Pynk is one of Nigeria's most exciting rising forces in music.
