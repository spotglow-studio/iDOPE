Dantiti

Afrobeats · Afrofusion · R&B · Rap

Nigerian-born, UK-raised. Daniel Ofuzor built his sound from Benin City to Wolverhampton, weaving Afrobeats, Afrofusion, and rap into something entirely his own. Inspired by Notorious B.I.G. as a kid in Nigeria, he carried that passion through Ghana and into the UK — where he founded iDOPE Records in 2017 to own his story from the start.

Our Story

About iDOPE

iDOPE Records is a creative hub founded by Daniel Ofuzor (Dantiti) in 2017, with a passion for music and mental wellness. We amplify bold voices, fresh sounds, and meaningful connections — aiming to reshape entertainment one beat at a time.

We aim to empower artists with innovative solutions, promote mental health, foster a culture where creativity and every voice are valued, and uphold ethics, sustainability, and social impact.
