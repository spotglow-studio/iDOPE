Who We Are

iDOPE is a record label and lifestyle brand based in London, United Kingdom. This website is operated by iDOPE. For any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, contact us at info@idoperecords.com.

Information We Collect

When you contact us by email, we receive the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We do not use cookies or tracking scripts that collect personal data on this site beyond standard, anonymised analytics that help us understand how the site is used.

How We Use Your Information

Any information you share with us is used solely to respond to your enquiry, manage bookings, press requests, or business correspondence. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Links

This site links to third-party platforms such as Spotify, YouTube, Instagram, and our merch store. Once you leave our site, your data is subject to the privacy policy of that third party. We encourage you to review their policies separately.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you at any time by emailing info@idoperecords.com.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
