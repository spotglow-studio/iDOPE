iDOPE Records · Artist

Dantiti

Afrobeats · Afrofusion · R&B · Rap

Nigerian-born, UK-raised. Daniel Ofuzor built his sound from Benin City to Wolverhampton, weaving Afrobeats, Afrofusion, and rap into something entirely his own. Inspired by Notorious B.I.G. as a kid in Nigeria, he carried that passion through Ghana and into the UK — where he founded iDOPE Records in 2017 to own his story from the start.
